Game Summary

Compete in a cosmic tournament as the outcast god, Pluto, in a quest to redeem your honor as a true planet and restore your rightful place among the stars. Fight other cosmic deities in card based RPG combat and manage and grow your set of celestial skills to aid you along your journey.

Pluto's Ascent was created during MassDiGI's Summer Innovation Program with five other developers.

Development Information

Role: Associate Producer, Programmer, 2D Artist
Company: MassDiGI
Tools: Unity, Photoshop, Plastic SCM, Visual Studio, Miro
Duration: May 2020 - August 2020
Team Size: 6
Genre:  Card Game
Platform: Mobile
Project Status: Completed - Coming Soon on Google Play and App Store!

Contributions

- Created AI for the enemies to be able to play the card game against the player.
- Developed systems for all enemies to be procedurally generated. Each enemy's statistics were produced randomly based on how far the player had gone on the map to maintain random but increasingly challenging gameplay.
- Made a modular system for customizing enemy names and portraits.
- Collaborated with teammates to create structures to save player data and manage information across scenes.
- Implemented player experience and leveling up.
- Concepted the cards used for base gameplay.
- Drew over 40 unique portraits for the different characters.
- Contributed to overall design of the gameplay mechanics and visual style.

This initial card concept was the basis of the art style for the rest of the game. All art followed the guidelines of simple lineart, clean coloring, and geometric styles.

Over 40 portraits were created with animated parts and two facial expressions. Each portrait was drawn with the pieces split apart to simplify the puppet rigged animation. Our concept artist designed the characters that I would draw and send back to her to be animated. Our pipeline was extremely quick with everyone playing to their strengths, which allowed us to create so many portraits in a very short period of time.
