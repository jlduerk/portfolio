Game Summary

In Sliptime Sleuth, play as a P.I. with a twist. In this isometric time-traveling puzzle game, utilize your handy gadget to take on a variety of contract work, slipping through time in order to earn an honest buck.

Sliptime Sleuth was displayed at various conventions and expos in MA, including PAX East 2019, BFiG 2019, and the Playcrafting Spring, Summer, and Halloween PLAY Expos.

Contributions

- Created 3D layouts of each level and assembled final models for each level.
- Concepted, modeled, and textured characters and environment objects.
- Implemented 3D art in Unity engine and created level-specific scripts for interactable objects.
- Managed the timeline for asset creation and ensured that art was delivered on time.
- Designed the overall interactable system that is used for each level.
- Collaborated with the rest of the team to design puzzles.

Working on Sliptime Sleuth gave me the opportunity to practice low-poly character and interior modeling. I also learned how to handle and organize a large set of assets so that art was completed by the deadlines. I worked with the designers to block out layouts for each scene, and then compiled the individual assets from other artists along with myself to create the final level formats.

Sliptime Sleuth also gave me the opportunity to work collaboratively as a programmer on the game. This taught me source control and how to split scripting work so that teammates were not overriding each other's progress.
